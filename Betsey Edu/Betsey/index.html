About Us


Embark on a journey with us through our rich history, steeped in a decade of successful early childhood development in Woodstock. Betsey Educare's roots trace back to a thriving center diligently overseen by our late mother-in-law, Louise Elizabeth Bruce. Her legacy endures as the baton was gracefully passed to Stephanie Esau, her daughter-in-law. Now based in Cape Town's CBD area, we proudly serve children from diverse backgrounds, ensuring they receive the best start in their educational journey.
Stephanie, equipped and inspired by her predecessor, envisions a vibrant Early Childhood Development (ECD) center that grants high-quality education, a supportive environment, and exemplary service. At Betsey Educare, we are driven by a passion to create a nurturing space where children can flourish. Our mission is to provide a fun, spacious learning environment that encourages exploration, authenticity, and the realization of their fullest potential.
Distinguishing ourselves from competitors, we seamlessly blend the principles of Montessori education with interactive learning experiences. Recognizing the diverse learning styles of children, our daily program caters to individual needs and strengths. Through passionate and engaging teaching, we aim to understand each child and offer unwavering support as they navigate their unique educational journey.

Our Services


After-School Care

Our after-school care program provides a safe and enriching environment where children can continue to learn and grow beyond regular school hours. We offer a variety of activities, including homework help, creative arts, sports, and STEM projects, all supervised by our caring staff. The program ensures each child receives personalized attention and the opportunity to explore new interests, fostering social development and lifelong friendships.

School holiday Programs

Our school holiday programs offer an exciting and enriching experience for children during their breaks. With a variety of activities, including creative arts, sports, educational workshops, and field trips, we provide a fun and stimulating environment where kids can explore new interests and make lasting memories. Supervised by our dedicated and experienced staff, each program is designed to ensure children are engaged, safe, and enjoying their time off from school to the fullest.

Tutoring

Our tutoring service is dedicated to helping students achieve academic success and build confidence in their abilities. With personalized one-on-one sessions and small group classes, we offer tailored support in a variety of subjects to meet each student's unique needs. Our experienced and passionate tutors employ effective teaching strategies to enhance understanding, improve grades, and foster a love for learning. Whether it's catching up or excelling further.
